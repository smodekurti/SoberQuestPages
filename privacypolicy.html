SoberQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 Aug 2025

SoberQuest is a personal wellness app. We respect your privacy.

Data Collection

  • No account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads.
  • App data (logs, goals, journal, preferences) is stored only on your device using local storage/SQLite.
  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not receive notification content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

Permissions

  • Notifications: optional; used for reminders you configure.

Your Choices

  • You can delete app data from within the app or by deleting the app.

Children’s Privacy

SoberQuest is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the “Last updated” date.
